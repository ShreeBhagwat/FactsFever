Why we don’t sell Ads?


I Started FactsFever 2 years ago as a telegram channel, where I and the other admins used to post facts. So that the subscribers could gain knowledge. At start it was just for fun and something to pass time. The channel grew fast and we did hit 10k Subs soon (Currently 25k and growing ). It was all the hard work of the team which gathered the facts around. Soon a time pass was changed into passion and we started feeling good for sharing facts.

Later did we realized that the so-called facts which were readily available are now getting scare and rare, as we had already uploaded the facts which were easy to find. Now it was our time to dive deep into the ocean to search for some of the rare and amazing facts which will truly amaze us. This was going well and we stated taking ads on our channel and grew faster.

But money was not the profit here. When we were searching facts to post on channel, unknowingly we gathered a lot of information. There were many things which we understood, which classes or tuitions could have never taught us. Our knowledge increased and we slowly felt that we must put these facts somewhere else so that we can reach more people with whom we can share our knowledge and dazzle them with the things going around the world.


This time I just started learning to code, as coding gave me satisfaction and this was my new hobby. I was a newbie and I happen to get a Mac which shifted my coding Language towards swift. I wanted to develop an app and I used to search on YouTube for tutorials and blogpost for App Development. At first I did not even know the basics of language and being a self-taught programmer it was hard to gets doubts cleared as there was no place to ask for, moreover my doubts were so silly that Stack Overflow would make fun of me. But with persistence and patience I had finally learned a a few things in app development.

To try out my skill and take a deep dive into the world of coding I decided to make a FactsFever app where I can post Facts and people will scroll thru the facts. This was just plain and simple workflow and layout. Where it will display images of facts and user can scroll to the next one. My only requirement to me was that this will be real time facts app. Normally the facts app present in the app store has their facts preset into the app, and for new facts you need to update your app. So, it was somewhere limited and I did now what this. So, I was successful

In creating this app and gave it for review. But Apple as we all know has strict Guideline laws, resulted in my app getting rejected and I had to add extra features and make it creative. This forced me to learn new things and ways of the coding world.


Here as a user I came to know one thing that is. No one wakes up excited to see more advertising, no one goes to sleep thinking about the ads they'll see tomorrow. We know people are excited to learn something new. To understand the confusing. Curiosity drives them I want FactsFever to be that part of your life that keeps you awake in search of knowledge... and that you reach for in the morning. No one jumps up from a nap and runs to see an advertisement.

So yes people ask me why are you not putting ads in this App, or what’s the benefits are you getting behind this app and many more questions. Little did they know that I have already received the benefits of this app. Which is more than the money I can ever get form the ads. Moreover, the main aim of this app to spread knowledge and information. Children of all ages are kept in mind and hence keeping this app free and ad free for them and me is must.
